## ğŸ¨ Brand Identity Guidelines

### Brand Positioning
- **Current (Omi):** General AI conversation recorder/transcriber for any purpose
- **New (Ella):** Personal health AI companion - caring, medical, supportive
- **Target Audience:** Patients, caregivers, healthcare consumers
- **Tone:** Professional yet warm, medical yet accessible, tech-forward yet human

### Color Palette Recommendations
Based on healthcare AI positioning:

**Primary Colors:**
- **Soft Medical Blue:** `#4A90E2` (trust, medical, calm)
- **Warm Care Pink:** `#E84A90` (care, warmth, human touch)
- **Clean White:** `#FFFFFF` (cleanliness, clarity)
- **Deep Navy:** `#1A2B4A` (professionalism, stability)

**Accent Colors:**
- **Healing Green:** `#2ECC71` (wellness, growth, health)
- **Soft Lavender:** `#9B59B6` (AI, tech, innovation)

**Backgrounds:**
- **Gradient Option 1:** Soft blue to lavender (medical tech)
- **Gradient Option 2:** Navy to teal (professional healthcare)
- **Gradient Option 3:** White to soft pink (gentle care)

### Typography
- **Logo Font:** Modern sans-serif (e.g., Poppins, Inter, SF Pro)
- **Tagline Font:** Clean, readable (e.g., Manrope, Open Sans)

### Logo Concepts

**Option 1: Abstract "E" Mark**
- Stylized "E" that resembles a speech bubble or waveform
- Represents conversation + AI
- Clean, minimal, modern

**Option 2: Heart + Waveform**
- Heart shape integrated with audio waveform
- Represents health + voice technology
- Warm, caring, tech-forward

**Option 3: Text-Based Wordmark**
- "Ella AI Care" in modern typography
- Possibly with small icon (microphone, heart, or pulse)
- Professional, medical-grade appearance

**Option 4: Nurse/Companion Icon**
- Simplified human figure with tech elements
- Represents AI companion/caregiver
- Approachable, human-centric
